Frequently Asked Questions

How does your multicurrency support work?

Our platform supports real-time currency conversion across 160+ currencies, automated exchange rate updates, and consolidated reporting in your preferred base currency. This ensures accurate financial tracking for international operations.

What security measures do you have in place?

We employ bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Our systems comply with international data protection standards and industry-specific regulations.

Can I integrate your services with my existing systems?

Yes, our platform offers seamless integration with major accounting software, ERP systems, and payment platforms through our API and native connectors.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 technical support, dedicated account managers for premium clients, and regular strategy sessions with our financial experts.

How quickly can we get started?

Most clients can be onboarded within 48 hours. Our team will guide you through the setup process and provide comprehensive training for your team.
